Sova Lineups

Sova is considered one of the highest skillcap agents due to the versatility his recon arrow brings to the team. His ability to have various charge strengths and bounces has allowed for multiple users to create lineups for cross-map plays and utility. Some of the core lineups are outlined on this page, divided by map and recon location.

As previously mentioned, Sova's arrow has various charges and their thresholds are marked by the vertical lines when the bow is pulled out. A charge of 0 will be when the blue bar is below the first line, then 1, 2, and 3 (full charge) The bounces are indicated by the diamonds located underneath the charge bar and can be toggled with the user's right mouse click.
