Killjoy Lineups

With Killjoy's kit, her abilities allow for easy post-plant setups which simply involve throwing a swarm grenade on the bomb before you leave. However, it becomes increasingly difficult to pull this off as you climb the ranks, so learning lineups can help win rounds when the enemy believes that the bomb is safe to defuse. The biggest caveat that comes with Killjoy's grenades is the fact that the enemy can shoot them as soon as they land, so be ready to pop them as soon as they drop!

Some of the lineups on this page will involve having your ultimate. While these opportunities will not always come, these can net your team some free kills and help win rounds easily! By pinching the enemy into a tight corner then throwing a grenade on them, they will be forced to either be detained by your ultimate or die to your swarm grenades.
