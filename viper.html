Viper Lineups

While Viper's primary use of smokes comes from her poison wall, there are certain maps where lineups for the smoke orb will come in handy. It is not always easy to run up and smoke off a chokepoint, so being safe from enemies and making sure the smokes are down properly is ensured through these lineups. However, once site is taken over (on attack side), it is recommended to pick up the smoke orb and place it on the bomb for the post-plant molotovs.

Due to Viper's kit, her ability to kill enemies with her smoke orb + molly combination prevents enemies from defusing the bomb greatly. There will be many cases where these lineups will win rounds and you won't even see an enemy the entire round! However, be careful as many players are aware of the Viper lineups and will search for you before they start defusing.
